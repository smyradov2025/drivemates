Owner Operators – FAQ

Answers to common questions about partnering with us.

What equipment do I need to start?

You’ll need a valid CDL, your own car hauler (3, 4, 5, 7, or 9 car capacity), DOT/MC number, and insurance coverage. We work with both experienced and new operators.

How and when do I get paid?

We offer weekly payments via direct deposit. Payment terms and rates are transparent and discussed during onboarding.

Can I choose my routes?

Yes! You can choose preferred regions or lanes. Our dispatch team works with your preferences and availability.
